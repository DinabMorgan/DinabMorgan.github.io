About Me

I've been told that I am a Renaissance woman. I have lots of interests, love learning and I have always been interested in tech. I remember when my father brought home the first computer for the family. I took to it like a moth to the light. When there were issues, I was the one that was fixing them. Soon I became the family tech person. Figuring out why the radio wasnt working, the TV was flickering, etc. My father even got me books on programming. However as life happens, I found other things I was intersted in as well and persued them, though I kept my love for computers in the background. Finally after a years of traveling, growing, learning I decided to persue the education needed to become a Software Developer.
